1. Introduction​

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, devices, and data from cyber threats.
In today’s digital world, almost everything is connected to the internet, which makes cybersecurity more important than ever.

Organizations rely on cybersecurity to protect:

  • People
  • Data
  • Devices
  • Systems

Cybersecurity helps ensure that information is safe from unauthorized access and attacks.

oaicite:0

2. What is Cybersecurity?​

Cybersecurity is a set of technologies, processes, and practices designed to protect digital systems.

It focuses on three main goals:

🔐 Confidentiality​

Ensures that only authorized users can access information.

🧾 Integrity​

Ensures that data is accurate and not modified.

⚙️ Availability​

Ensures that systems and data are accessible when needed.

👉 This is known as the CIA Triad.

3. Why Cybersecurity is Important​

Cyber attacks are increasing every day, and they can cause serious damage.

Some impacts include:

  • Data breaches
  • Financial loss
  • Reputation damage
  • System downtime

Cybersecurity helps organizations prevent and respond to these risks.

4. Common Cyber Threats​

A threat is anything that can harm a system or data.

🔥 Examples of threats:​

  • Malware
  • Phishing attacks
  • Ransomware
  • Social engineering

A threat actor is the person or group behind the attack.

oaicite:1

5. Cybersecurity Around Us​

Cybersecurity is everywhere in daily life:

  • Online banking
  • Social media
  • Email accounts
  • E-commerce

Every time you use the internet, cybersecurity plays a role in protecting your data.

6. Careers in Cybersecurity​

Cybersecurity is a high-demand field with many job roles.

Some common roles:

  • Security Analyst
  • SOC Analyst (Security Operations Center)
  • Incident Responder
  • Threat Analyst

Cybersecurity professionals are responsible for monitoring systems, detecting threats, and responding to attacks.

7. Key Skills in Cybersecurity​

To work in cybersecurity, you need:

  • Analytical thinking
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Basic knowledge of networks
  • Understanding of threats and vulnerabilities

8. Summary​

In this module, we learned:

  • What cybersecurity is
  • The importance of cybersecurity
  • The CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability)
  • Common threats and threat actors
  • Career opportunities in cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is an essential field in today’s digital world, and it will continue to grow in the future.
